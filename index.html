The Profile

Built from the ground up.
On purpose.

Mariam Serra is the Founder of Avenue Homes, a company built not from ambition alone, but from a deep conviction about what real estate leadership should look like. Before a license, before a brokerage, there was a student: attentive, driven, learning the craft from the inside as an assistant to her broker.

Growing up, Mariam learned early that stability is earned and that the strongest people are often the ones who choose responsibility before it is convenient. Those formative years shaped how she leads today: calm under pressure, protective of her people, and committed to doing things the right way even when it is harder.

That foundation paid off immediately. In her very first year with a license, Mariam was named Top Producer at her brokerage. Then she did it again in year two. Consecutive recognition that had nothing to do with luck and everything to do with character.

When that brokerage was sold to a larger corporation, she did not follow. In 2019, she founded Avenue Homes, the brokerage she once needed, now built entirely on her own terms.

"To lead with heart, empower those around you, and always put people first."
— Mariam Serra, on her mission
