Profile

Her story

Mariam Serra is the Founder and Broker of Avenue Homes, bringing a decade of real estate experience rooted in excellence, resilience, and heart.

Before launching Avenue Homes in 2019, Mariam was recognized as a top producer two years in a row at her previous brokerage, a reflection of both her work ethic and the care she brings to each client relationship.

When that brokerage was sold to a larger corporation, she stepped into brokerage leadership with a clear intention: to build the kind of company she once needed herself.

Lead with heart. Protect the client. Elevate the standard.

Avenue Homes leadership philosophy

Leadership

Craft, competence, and character

Mariam’s real estate journey began before she was licensed, first as an assistant to her former broker and later as a standout student of the craft. Today, she is known for both deep industry knowledge and steady, ethical guidance through high-stakes decisions.

Mission

A simple mission, lived daily

Mariam’s mission is simple: to lead with heart, empower her agents, and always put people first. In a market that moves fast, her approach stays grounded in clarity, honesty, and calm leadership.

She believes the strongest businesses are built on trust, and that a broker’s job is to raise the standard for service, professionalism, and protection at every step of the transaction.
