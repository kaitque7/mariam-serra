The Profile

Built from the ground up.
On purpose.

Mariam Serra is the Founder of Avenue Homes, a company built not from ambition alone, but from a deep conviction about what real estate leadership should look like. Before a license, before a brokerage, there was a student: attentive, driven, learning the craft from the inside as an assistant to her broker.

Growing up, Mariam learned early that stability is earned and that the strongest people are often the ones who choose responsibility before it is convenient. Those formative years shaped how she leads today: calm under pressure, protective of her people, and committed to doing things the right way even when it is harder.

That foundation paid off immediately. In her very first year with a license, Mariam was named Top Producer at her brokerage. Then she did it again in year two. Consecutive recognition that had nothing to do with luck and everything to do with character.

When that brokerage was sold to a larger corporation, she did not follow. In 2019, she founded Avenue Homes, the brokerage she once needed, now built entirely on her own terms.

The Journey

Her Story

Before Mariam Serra ever held a real estate license, she was already in the business, absorbing it from the inside, working as an assistant to her broker, learning not just the mechanics of transactions but the human dimensions of them: the weight of a client's trust, the responsibility of guiding someone through one of the most significant decisions of their life.

That same perspective was shaped long before real estate. Her upbringing taught her to value consistency, to protect her name, and to lead with a steady hand. It is why clients feel safe with her, and why agents feel supported around her.

In her first year licensed, she was named Top Producer, then again in year two. Back to back. Not a moment, a pattern.

"The kind of leadership you can feel: steady, intentional, and built on trust."

When her brokerage was sold to a larger corporation, Mariam did not adapt to someone else's vision. She stepped into brokerage leadership and built her own. Avenue Homes opened in 2019, not as a corporate satellite, but as a brokerage with a soul: the kind of company Mariam once needed herself, now brought to life on her own terms.
